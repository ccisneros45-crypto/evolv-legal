Evolv Privacy Policy Last updated: 06-17-2026 Evolv ("the app," "we," "our") is a weight loss and health journey tracking app. This policy explains what information Evolv collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. We wrote this in plain language because we believe a health app's privacy policy should be as easy to read as the app itself. The short version Evolv is built local-first. Your photos, weight history, health data, and journal entries live on your device. Nothing leaves your phone automatically. The only time any of your data is sent anywhere is when you explicitly choose to use the AI Insights feature or the Medical Export feature, and both require your direct action and confirmation before anything is sent. What Evolv collects and stores on your device When you use Evolv, the following information is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage: Progress photos you take or import Weight entries Health data you choose to sync from Apple Health, including steps, sleep, resting heart rate, blood pressure, and protein intake Medication and peptide tracking information you enter, including dose logs, side effects, and notes Check-in details such as hunger and food noise ratings Profile information you provide, such as age, biological sex, or goal weight, if entered This information is not visible to us. We do not have a server that stores your photos, your weight history, or your day-to-day entries. It stays on your device and in your iCloud backup if you have that enabled through Apple, the same as any other app data on your phone. Apple Health If you grant Evolv access to Apple Health, we read only the specific data types you approve, such as steps, sleep, resting heart rate, blood pressure, and protein. Apple manages these permissions, and you can review or revoke them at any time in the Settings app under Privacy & Security > Health. Evolv does not write data back to Apple Health except for the data types you've explicitly enabled syncing for, if applicable. AI Insights feature Evolv includes an optional AI Insights feature. When you tap to generate an Insight, Evolv prepares a summary of your numeric and text data — such as recent weight trends, sleep averages, step counts, and notes you've written — and asks for your confirmation before sending it. No photos are ever included in this data package. If you confirm, that summary is sent to our backend server, which forwards it to a third-party AI provider to generate a response. The response is returned to your device and displayed in the app. We do not store this data on our server beyond what's needed to process the request and return a response to you. Your data is not used to train AI models. You can use Evolv fully without ever using this feature. It is entirely optional and requires your explicit action and confirmation every time. Medical Export feature Evolv includes a Medical Export feature that prepares a plain-text summary of your data, intended for use with an AI assistant of your choosing or for a clinician visit. This export is shown to you for review before anything happens. Evolv does not send this export anywhere automatically — you choose to copy it and paste it into whatever tool or context you prefer. We have no visibility into what you do with it after you copy it. Data you delete If you delete an entry, photo, or your account data within the app, that information is removed from your device. Since we don't store a copy of your personal health data on our servers, there is nothing further for us to delete on our end beyond the limited request logs described above, which are not linked to your identity. Children's privacy Evolv is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
